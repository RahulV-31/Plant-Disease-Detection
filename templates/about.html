About Plant Disease Detective

Our Mission

Plant Disease Detective aims to empower farmers, gardeners, and plant enthusiasts with AI-powered technology to quickly identify plant diseases and implement effective treatments.

By providing accurate disease detection and tailored recommendations, we help increase crop yields, reduce pesticide use, and promote sustainable agricultural practices.

The Technology Behind Plant Disease Detective

Our platform uses a convolutional neural network (CNN) trained on thousands of plant images to identify 33 different types of plant diseases across various crops including:

Model Performance

Our deep learning model achieves over 95% accuracy in detecting plant diseases across our test dataset. The model was trained using data augmentation techniques to ensure robustness across different lighting conditions, angles, and backgrounds.
